Season Days Per Year (Dec–Nov) — Years on Vertical Axis
Methodology — Season years run December through November (e.g. "2024–25" = Dec 2024–Nov 2025). Daily mean = (high+low)/2. Thresholds are computed per station from 1981–2010 normals: the coldest and warmest day-of-year averages are found, and the range is split into four equal quarters. Summer = top 25%. Winter = bottom 25%. Spring/Autumn = middle 50%, split Jan 16 / Jul 16. Original methodology: joshtimlin.com/seasons.
